# OctoAcme — Role Accountability Template

Use this template in initiation, planning, and milestone reviews to make ownership and handoffs explicit.

## Decision and Ownership Log

| Area / Decision | Accountable (A) | Responsible (R) | Consulted (C) | Informed (I) | Due Date | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example: Scope approval for Milestone 1 | Product Lead | PM, Developers | BA, UX, QA | Stakeholder Champions | YYYY-MM-DD | Open |
| Example: Release go/no-go | PM | QA Lead, Developers | PdM, Support | Sponsor | YYYY-MM-DD | Open |

## Handoff Checklist (Per Phase)

- [ ] Source artifacts are complete (requirements, designs, test evidence, runbooks)
- [ ] Receiving owner is named and has confirmed acceptance
- [ ] Open risks/dependencies and mitigations are documented
- [ ] Decision log entry is recorded for the handoff
- [ ] Stakeholders to inform are listed with communication channel/date
